Classic Chocolate Chip Tartare

Soft, buttery cookie dough with rich chocolate pockets and a warm brown sugar finish made for spoonfuls and dramatic dessert moments.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1/2 cup Ghirardelli milk chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup Hershey's mini chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Heat-treat the flour by microwaving it for 1 minute and 15 seconds, stirring every 15 seconds.
  3. Beat the sugar and butter until creamy.
  4. Beat in the vanilla and salt, then mix in the flour until a crumbly dough forms.
  5. Stir in the milk, then fold in both chocolate chip varieties.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
