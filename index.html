Salted Caramel Swirl

Brown butter dough, caramel ribbons, and flaky sea salt served in a way that feels worthy of the dessert course.

  • Use browned butter for deeper flavor.
  • Fold in a soft caramel ribbon before chilling.
  • Finish with flaky salt just before serving.

Espresso Brownie Bite

Chocolate-forward dough with espresso and dark chocolate shards designed for elegant spoonfuls and dramatic finales.

  • Bloom espresso powder for a bolder finish.
  • Use a mix of cocoa and melted chocolate.
  • Chill until the center stays rich and fudgy.

Birthday Cake Fantasy

Vanilla-rich dough with candy pieces and frosting-like flair made for playful tableside presentation.

  • Use vanilla bean paste for extra aroma.
  • Add colorful sprinkles for a festive finish.
  • Serve chilled with a light frosting drizzle.
